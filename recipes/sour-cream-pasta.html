Sour Cream Pasta

Description

Classic, simple Hungarian dish according to my wife. Won't impress guests but will do the job.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Sour cream - if stranded in France, Creme Fraiche or Fromage Blanc can be used as a replacement but do try them first as they are quite different.
  • Grated cheese
  • Garlic/Garlic powder

Steps

  • Boil the pasta in your favorite pasta-boiling pot. Salt and oil to taste although oil is supposed to be useless - you do you, though. Don't forget to drain the pasta.
  • Add the Sour Cream (one spoonful, two spoonfuls or more, up to you) on steaming hot pasta, add salt, pepper and garlic/garlic powder to taste.
  • Sprinkle the grated cheese to taste.
  • Mix, or don't.
  • Dig in, and jó étvágyat!
